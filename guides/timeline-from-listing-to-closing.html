Timeline from listing to closing

Most sales follow a familiar rhythm. Use this outline to plan prep, showings, negotiation, and closing so your move feels calm and organized.

Prep and launch

  • One to two weeks for light prep, photos, and copy.
  • Listing live by a mid week launch for weekend traffic.
  • Track early metrics and adjust quickly if needed.

Under contract period

  • Inspection, appraisal, and financing milestones with clear dates.
  • Repair negotiations or credits if needed.
  • Title work and lender conditions cleared on time.

Closing week

  1. Final walk through and confirmations.
  2. Utilities and insurance updates set for the right day.
  3. Keys and possession plan documented.
