Vacant vs occupied showing strategy

Vacant listings show quickly and stay cleaner. Occupied listings can work with a tight plan. Here is how to decide and keep momentum either way.

When vacant wins

  • Fast access for tours, appraisals, and repairs.
  • Easy to stage, clean, and keep photo ready.
  • Best for relocation timelines or homes needing work.

When occupied works

  • Strong school or commute windows where timing is critical.
  • Simple homes that photograph well and are easy to keep tidy.
  • Flexible showing blocks each day and weekend open houses.

Momentum tips

  1. Use a showing window strategy and daily cleaning checklist.
  2. Crate pets or arrange off site care during showings.
  3. Refresh lead photo weekly and track saves and feedback trends.
