Closing day for sellers

You’ll sign transfer documents, confirm funds, and deliver possession as agreed. Here’s the quick overview so nothing gets missed.

Before you arrive

  • Complete agreed repairs and save receipts.
  • Gather keys, garage remotes, pool and mailbox info.
  • Schedule utilities to end the day after closing.

At the table

Sign the deed and seller documents, verify payoff figures, and confirm how your proceeds will be sent. Many closings are now mobile or remote-online if allowed.

Possession timing

Some contracts allow post-closing occupancy or rent-back. Put dates, payments, and insurance clearly in writing to protect both sides.

Need a clean handoff plan

An experienced listing agent coordinates title, movers, and buyer access so the handover is smooth.
