When to start working with a Realtor

The short answer is early. A quick call helps align budget, neighborhoods, and timelines so you focus only on homes that fit. That keeps stress low and results strong.

What happens when you start early

  • Real pricing for your city and must have features.
  • Introductions to a lender who fits your score and down payment.
  • Clear plan for touring, making offers, and timing a move.

Signs it is time to connect

  • You are six to twelve weeks from wanting to tour homes.
  • You need to coordinate a lease end, sale, or job change.
  • You want to avoid common first time buyer mistakes.
