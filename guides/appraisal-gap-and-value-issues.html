Appraisal gap and value issues

A low appraisal is common when demand is strong. You still have options. The goal is to close at a fair number with minimal delay.

Why appraisals come in low

  • Lack of recent comparable sales at your price point.
  • Rapidly changing market or unique property features.
  • Condition notes that require repairs for loan approval.

Paths forward

  • Buyer brings cash to cover part or all of the gap.
  • Price adjustment that keeps both sides on track.
  • Provide stronger comps or updates for a reconsideration.

Protect your net

  1. Review how your contract treats appraisal shortfalls.
  2. Weigh days on market if you return to active status.
  3. Stay factual and document any recent upgrades or permits.
