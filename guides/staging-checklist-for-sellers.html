Staging checklist for sellers

Staging helps buyers imagine themselves in the space. Small changes often have the biggest effect.

Declutter and depersonalize

  • Remove excess furniture to open space.
  • Box up personal photos and collections.
  • Clear counters and closets by half.

Light and freshness

Clean windows, open curtains, and add fresh plants or flowers to make every room inviting.

Final touches

  • Set dining tables neatly.
  • Add fresh towels in bathrooms.
  • Play soft background music for showings.
