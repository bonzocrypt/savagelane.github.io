HOA and condo basics

Communities with associations can be great when you understand the rules and costs. Here is what to review before you offer.

What dues usually cover

  • Exterior maintenance, landscaping, or amenities.
  • Master insurance for shared structures or common areas.
  • Reserves for future repairs and replacements.

Rules and restrictions to check

  • Leasing limits, pet policies, vehicle and parking rules.
  • Architectural controls for exterior changes.
  • Short term rental policies if that is a future plan.

Documents worth reading

  • Budget and reserve study to gauge financial health.
  • Recent meeting minutes for upcoming projects or issues.
  • Insurance summary to understand coverage and gaps.
