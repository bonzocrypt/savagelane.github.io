FHA vs Conventional vs VA vs USDA

Loan types exist to fit different buyers and locations. Use these quick contrasts to see what might fit, then confirm the details with a local lender.

Who each loan is built for

  • FHA Flexible credit and lower down payment with mortgage insurance.
  • Conventional Stronger credit and more options to remove PMI.
  • VA Eligible veterans and service members with no down payment and no PMI.
  • USDA Eligible rural areas with income limits and no down payment.

Down payment and insurance at a glance

  • FHA As low as three point five percent down, upfront and monthly mortgage insurance.
  • Conventional As low as three percent down, PMI can be removed later.
  • VA Often zero down with a funding fee, no monthly mortgage insurance.
  • USDA Zero down with a guarantee fee and area eligibility rules.

How to choose

  1. List your credit score, debts, and target price.
  2. Check eligibility for VA or USDA first if you qualify.
  3. Compare total monthly and cash to close across two or three options.
