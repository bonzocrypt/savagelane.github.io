Moving checklist for sellers

A steady plan reduces stress. Use this schedule to hand off keys with confidence and avoid last minute scrambles.

Four weeks out

  • Book movers or a rental truck. Start gathering boxes and labels.
  • Sort and donate items you will not move.
  • Confirm closing and possession dates with your agent.

One week out

  • Change address with USPS and key accounts.
  • Schedule utilities to end the day after closing.
  • Prepare a small open first box for each room.

Closing week

Complete any agreed repairs, clean floors and surfaces, and leave manuals, spare keys, and codes for the buyers.
