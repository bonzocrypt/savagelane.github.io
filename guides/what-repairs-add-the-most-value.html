What repairs add the most value

Spend where it counts. Buyers and appraisers value safety, working systems, and clean design. Small updates often outperform big remodels right before a sale.

High impact and low waste

  • Fresh paint, modern lighting, and new cabinet hardware.
  • Simple curb appeal like trimmed landscaping and clean entry.
  • Minor bath refresh such as new fixtures and regrout where needed.

Repair risks that can sink deals

  • Roof leaks, active plumbing leaks, or unsafe electrical issues.
  • HVAC that does not cool or heat properly.
  • Wood rot and moisture problems that show up in inspections.

How to choose projects

  1. Fix safety and system issues first.
  2. Prioritize updates that show in photos and first impressions.
  3. Get quotes so you can weigh repair credits versus doing the work.
