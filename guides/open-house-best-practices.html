Open house best practices

A good open house builds momentum for private showings. Make it easy to visit and easy to remember.

Before the event

  • Announce dates early and share clear parking info.
  • Secure valuables and medication. Use a sign in.
  • Set a friendly path through the home with doors open and lights on.

During

Welcome visitors, answer questions, and hand out a one page highlight sheet. Keep the focus on the home rather than sales talk.

After

  • Follow up with all buyers who requested details.
  • Ask your agent what comments repeated across visitors.
  • Decide if any quick tweaks could improve the next week’s activity.
