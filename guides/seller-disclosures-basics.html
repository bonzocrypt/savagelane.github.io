Seller disclosures basics

Disclosures protect both you and the buyer. Share known material facts about the property and keep copies of any repairs or permits.

Common disclosure topics

  • Roof leaks, water intrusion, or prior flooding
  • Foundation, electrical, or plumbing issues
  • Mold, pests, or environmental concerns
  • Improvements done with or without permits

Tip

If you don’t know an answer, say so honestly. Guessing can cause problems later.

Best practices

  • Fill forms completely and legibly.
  • Attach receipts, warranties, and permits.
  • Update disclosures if something changes before closing.
