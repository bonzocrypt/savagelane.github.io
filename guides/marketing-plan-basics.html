Marketing plan basics

A clear plan brings more showings and better offers. Focus on speed to market, strong visuals, and targeted exposure.

Core elements

  • Pro photography and floor plan with dimensions.
  • Mobile friendly listing copy with clear highlights.
  • Launch timing that matches local buyer activity.
  • Wide syndication and paid boosts where useful.

Pro tip

Use a short teaser before the live date to build early saves, then announce when showings open with a clear schedule.
