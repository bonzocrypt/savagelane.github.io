FSBO vs agent

Selling yourself can work in some cases. Hiring a pro can bring wider reach, better screening, and fewer surprises. Use this quick compare to choose.

When FSBO can fit

  • You have strong time and comfort with paperwork.
  • You can be home for showings and negotiation.
  • The property is simple with clear comps.

When an agent adds value

  • Busy schedule or limited local market knowledge.
  • Homes that need marketing, staging, or tricky repairs.
  • Multiple offers, appraisal risk, or complex timelines.

Reality check

Most buyers work with an agent. A listing agent handles access, safety, screening, contracts, and deadlines so you keep control without the stress.
