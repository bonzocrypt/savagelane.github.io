Home inspection and appraisal basics

The inspection protects you from hidden issues. The appraisal protects your lender from overpaying. Use both to make a smart decision and keep your deal on track.

What the inspection covers

  • Roof, structure, electrical, plumbing, and HVAC condition.
  • Function of major systems and safety concerns.
  • Optional specialty checks like sewer, pool, termite, or mold.

What the appraisal does

  • Confirms market value based on similar recent sales.
  • Notes condition items that can affect loan eligibility.
  • Is required by most lenders for financed purchases.

Handling issues the smart way

  1. Prioritize safety and major system repairs over cosmetic items.
  2. Request seller repairs or credits based on professional quotes.
  3. If value comes in low, discuss options like price change, more cash, or appraisal rebuttal.
