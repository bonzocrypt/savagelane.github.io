Buyer credit basics and quick wins

A stronger score can help with approval and cost. Below are simple steps that many buyers follow before they apply.

Focus on the big factors

On time payments and credit use carry the most weight. Set calendar reminders or auto pay so nothing is missed. Keep balances low relative to limits. Mid cycle payments can lower reported balances.

Clean up clear errors

Check each bureau report for wrong late marks or balances. Dispute only items that are clearly incorrect and keep copies of responses.

Do not open many new accounts at once

Multiple new accounts can lower age of credit. If you must open a line for a need, avoid stacking new inquiries right before a mortgage review.
