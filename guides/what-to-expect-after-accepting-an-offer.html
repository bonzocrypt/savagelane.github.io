What to expect after accepting an offer

Once you’re under contract, several checkpoints must clear before closing. Here’s the timeline and what to prepare for.

Typical steps before closing

  • Buyer schedules inspection — repairs may be requested.
  • Lender orders appraisal and finalizes loan approval.
  • Title company clears title and prepares documents.
  • Both sides confirm closing date and moving arrangements.

Tip

Keep utilities active until the day after closing and make all agreed repairs early to avoid last-minute stress.
