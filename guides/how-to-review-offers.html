How to review offers

When offers come in, price isn’t the only factor. Consider strength of financing, contingencies, and timing before accepting.

Evaluate core factors

  • Purchase price and net after concessions
  • Financing type and down payment strength
  • Contingencies (inspection, appraisal, home sale)
  • Closing timeline and possession date

Tip

A cash offer with fewer contingencies may beat a higher financed offer with more risk of delay.
