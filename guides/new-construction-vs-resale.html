New construction vs resale

New builds offer warranties and customization. Resales offer established neighborhoods and mature lots. Here is how to compare the real cost and timeline.

What you get with new construction

  • Builder warranties on structure, systems, and workmanship.
  • Modern layouts, energy codes, and fewer early repairs.
  • Longer timelines and potential premiums for lots and upgrades.

What you get with resale

  • Established areas, shade trees, and finished landscaping.
  • Room to negotiate on price, closing costs, or repairs.
  • Older systems or cosmetic updates you may do over time.

Compare the real number

  1. Price plus premiums: lot, elevation, and structural options.
  2. Design center picks: flooring, cabinets, counters, lighting.
  3. After close items: blinds, fridge, washer, dryer, backyard.
