First time buyer mistakes to avoid

You do not need to be perfect to buy a home. Avoid these common traps and your search and closing will be much calmer.

Mistakes that raise the payment

  • Ignoring taxes, HOA, and insurance in your budget.
  • Opening new credit or large purchases before closing.
  • Skipping quotes for flood, wind, or condo insurance.

Mistakes that slow or risk closing

  • Missing contract timelines for inspections and loan approval.
  • Weak pre approval or choosing the wrong loan type for your profile.
  • Asking for cosmetic fixes rather than safety and system items.

Simple wins

  1. Get a strong pre approval and price range before touring.
  2. Use a clear checklist for showings and inspection priorities.
  3. Keep your finances quiet until the keys are in hand.
