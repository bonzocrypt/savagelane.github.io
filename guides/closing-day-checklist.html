Closing day checklist

Closing day is busy but predictable. Use this short checklist to avoid last minute surprises and start your move in smoothly.

What to bring

  • Government issued ID for all signers.
  • Closing funds by wire or certified check, as instructed by your title company.
  • Proof of homeowner insurance and any required documents from your lender.

What to double check

  • Final walkthrough completed and any agreed repairs documented.
  • Names, address, and loan terms on the closing disclosure match your agreement.
  • Keys, garage remotes, mailbox info, and HOA details are ready for handoff.

After you sign

  1. Confirm the deed is recorded and funds are disbursed before moving in.
  2. Change locks and set up utilities if not already scheduled.
  3. Store closing documents in a safe place and set reminders for homestead and tax exemptions if applicable.
