How to price your home correctly

The right price attracts the right buyers. Here is how pros weigh comps, condition, and current demand to set a number that supports strong terms.

What goes into the price

  • Recent comparable sales within a tight radius and similar specs.
  • Adjustments for upgrades, lot, and unique features.
  • Market velocity and months of supply in your price band.

Smart pricing strategies

  • Price to the bracket buyers search most often.
  • Leave room for strong terms rather than chasing a vanity number.
  • Watch first week feedback and adjust if showings are light.

Quick sanity checks

  1. Your home should appear in the same searches as the most similar sales.
  2. Photos match the promise of the price.
  3. Your price makes sense if the appraisal uses the same comps.
