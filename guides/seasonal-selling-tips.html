Seasonal selling tips

Buyers move year-round. Highlight different features by season and keep your listing feeling fresh in photos and in person.

Spring and summer

  • Fresh mulch, trimmed shrubs, and colorful planters.
  • Show off outdoor living with clean furniture and string lights.
  • Schedule photos on a bright day; open blinds for maximum light.

Fall and winter

  • Warm lighting and cozy textiles for inviting rooms.
  • Keep walks clear, leaves raked, and gutters cleaned.
  • Use early afternoon showings for best natural light.

Tip

Swap your hero photos seasonally if your home stays on market longer than expected. Fresh visuals re-engage saved searches.
