Photography and showing prep

Great photos and smooth showings start with a simple plan. Focus on light, space, and clean lines. Your goal is to help buyers imagine living here.

Photo day checklist

  • Open blinds and curtains. Turn on every light.
  • Clear kitchen and bath counters. Hide trash cans.
  • Make beds. Smooth linens and pillows.
  • Park cars away from the house. Hide hoses and bins.
  • Wipe mirrors, glass, and stainless steel.

Timing tip

Book photos when your home gets the best natural light. Ask for a few dusk shots if outdoor lighting looks great at night.

Before each showing

  • Do a five minute sweep. Lights on, blinds set, floors clear.
  • Set the temperature to feel comfortable.
  • Secure pets and remove signs of litter boxes or bowls.
  • Leave a simple flyer with highlights buyers might miss.
