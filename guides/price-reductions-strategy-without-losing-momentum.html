Price reductions strategy without losing momentum

A smart price change is a reset, not a surrender. Use the first weeks of data to decide when and where to move so you land in the right buyer pool.

Read the signals

  • Views and saves are soft relative to similar listings.
  • Showings are light for your segment and price range.
  • Feedback points to a value gap rather than fixable issues.

Make a purposeful move

  • Cross a price band so new buyers see the update in their alerts.
  • Update the lead photo and first three lines of copy the same day.
  • Have your agent call top buyer agents to promote the change.

What to avoid

  1. Small changes that do not reach a new buyer pool.
  2. Too many changes in a short period that signal confusion.
  3. Waiting so long that a better positioned listing wins the market.
