Repairs and updates that pay off

Some updates make your home show beautifully without draining your budget. Here’s where sellers see the best returns.

High-impact, low-cost fixes

  • Fresh neutral paint and clean trim.
  • Updated light fixtures and hardware.
  • Mulched landscaping and pressure-washed walkways.

Worth considering

Minor kitchen or bath refreshes often recoup 80–90% of cost if done with modern but modest materials.

Skip these unless outdated or damaged

  • Full roof replacement when lifespan remains.
  • Luxury remodels beyond neighborhood range.
  • High-end landscaping right before listing.
