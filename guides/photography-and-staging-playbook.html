Photography and staging playbook

Great images and simple staging drive clicks and showings. Use this quick plan to make every room feel bright, spacious, and welcoming.

Prep before shoot day

  • Declutter counters and floors and hide cords where you can.
  • Open blinds and curtains and replace dim bulbs.
  • Fresh towels, a plant, and two simple accent pieces per room.

Must have shots

  • Front exterior, entry, living, kitchen, main suite, and best extra space.
  • Backyard or balcony and community features that buyers value.
  • Floor plan or simple layout graphic helps buyers orient quickly.

Listing polish

  1. Use the best exterior as the lead and test a second lead image day two.
  2. Caption upgrades and recent improvements in the first photos.
  3. Keep twelve to twenty four images so buyers want the full tour link.
