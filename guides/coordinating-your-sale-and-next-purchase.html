Coordinating your sale and next purchase

Selling and buying at the same time is doable with a plan. Align dates, choose the right financing, and keep backups ready so you can move once.

Timing options

  • Close both homes the same day with a clear possession plan.
  • Rent back after closing to avoid a double move.
  • Short term housing if the new home needs work first.

Financing tools

  • Bridge loan or HELOC to unlock equity for the next down payment.
  • Contingent offer with strong terms and short timelines.
  • Sell first and buy with cash or conventional financing next.

Keep stress low

  1. Plan move dates early and reserve movers.
  2. Line up utility transfers and insurance updates.
  3. Have a backup property list in case Plan A changes.
