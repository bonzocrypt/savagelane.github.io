Making an offer with confidence

A clear process helps you write a strong offer without overpaying. Your agent runs the details so you focus on the big decisions.

How price is decided

  • Recent comparable sales adjusted for size, condition, and location.
  • Current competition and days on market.
  • Your comfort range based on payment and cash to close.

Terms that matter

  • Earnest money, inspection and financing timelines, and appraisal language.
  • Seller credits, personal property, and possession timing.
  • Communication tone and completeness of your package.

What your agent handles behind the scenes

  • Pulling comps, calling the listing agent, and reading seller signals.
  • Coordinating with your lender for a strong pre approval letter.
  • Drafting clean paperwork and managing deadlines after acceptance.
