Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Who we are

Savage Lane operates a referral concierge for real estate. We act in a referral only capacity. After you submit our form we register your request with our brokerage referral program so a licensed sales professional can contact you about your real estate needs. We do not list, show, or negotiate real estate.

What we collect

  • Contact details such as name, email, phone
  • Location such as ZIP or city and state
  • Basic intent such as buying, selling, or both plus optional timeline and notes

How we collect it

Information is collected when you submit the form on savagelane.com. Form handling is provided by Formspree which securely relays submissions to our email and dashboard.

How we use information

  • To review your request and confirm we received it
  • To register your request with our brokerage referral program for placement with a qualified licensed sales professional
  • To stay in touch about status and service quality

Sharing

We share your details only as needed to register and place your referral with the brokerage referral program and the assigned licensed sales professional. We do not sell personal information.

Data retention

We keep referral records for as long as needed for placement, follow up, and compliance. If you ask us to delete your information we will do so unless we must keep it for legal or record keeping reasons.

Your choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information
  • You may request that we stop contacting you except for required notices

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.

Cookies and analytics

We do not use analytics or advertising cookies on this site at this time.

Children

This service is for adults and is not directed to children under 18.

Contact

For privacy requests email hello@savagelane.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
