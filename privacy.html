Savage Lane Home Match Concierge respects your privacy. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. Savage Lane participates solely as a referral associate through the Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network. We operate strictly in a referral only capacity and do not perform brokerage services. We do not list, show, or negotiate real estate. Matches are made to licensed professionals in your market through the brokerage referral program, where permitted by law. All referrals are placed under the Independent Contractor Agreement and related brokerage policies and addenda.

Information we collect

  • Contact details, such as your name, email, and phone, when you submit the form.
  • Home goals, such as city, price range, and timing, that you share in the form.
  • Technical data that your browser provides, such as IP address and user agent, for security and site performance.

How we use information

  • To respond to your request and confirm details for your referral.
  • To place a referral through the Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network and connect you with a qualified licensed real estate professional.
  • To improve our site and safeguard against fraud or misuse.

Sharing

We share your information only as needed to process your referral through the Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network and to connect you with the assigned professional. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to service your request and meet our obligations, including recordkeeping required by the referral network and applicable policies.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to legal and contractual limits.
  • You can contact us to update your preferences or withdraw your request before a referral is placed.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, but we work to protect your data and review safeguards over time.

Form submissions

Our contact form is powered by Formspree. Form content you submit is transmitted to us so we can respond. For questions about how Formspree processes data, review their documentation.

Referral and brokerage disclosure

Savage Lane participates solely as a referral associate through the Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network. We operate strictly in a referral only capacity. After you submit a request, we register your information with our brokerage referral program so an assigned licensed sales professional can contact you regarding your real estate needs. We do not list, show, or negotiate property. Matches are made to licensed professionals in your market through the brokerage referral program, where permitted by law.

Savage Lane does not provide legal, tax, or financial advice. Any real estate services are provided exclusively by the licensed real estate professionals to whom your request is referred, under their brokerage and local law. Your decision to work with any referred professional is entirely your choice.

Contact

To ask questions or exercise a privacy right, reply to your confirmation email or reach us through the contact form on the home page.
