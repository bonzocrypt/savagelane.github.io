Privacy Policy

Effective date:

This Privacy Policy explains what information Savage Lane collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Savage Lane is a referral introduction website. No real estate services or representation are provided through this website. If you request an introduction, your information may be shared with an appropriate licensed real estate professional for brokerage services.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as your name, email, and phone when you submit the referral request form
  • Request details such as location, timeframe, and basic preferences you choose to share in the form
  • Technical data such as IP address, device information, and browser data for security and site performance

How we use information

  • To process and confirm your referral request
  • To place a referral through the brokerage referral program and connect you with a licensed professional
  • To improve site reliability and protect against misuse

Sharing

We share your information only as needed to process your referral request and connect you with the assigned licensed professional. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

We keep information as long as needed to service your request and meet recordkeeping obligations required by applicable policies and law.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to legal and contractual limits
  • You can withdraw your request before a referral is placed

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, but we work to protect data and review safeguards over time.

Form submissions

Referral requests may be collected using a form service. Information you submit is transmitted to us so we can process your request and place the referral.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email privacy@savagelane.com. If you submitted a referral request, you can also respond to any confirmation message you received.

Disclosure: Savage Lane is an independent referral introduction website and is not operated by Coldwell Banker Realty. As a Referral Agent, I do not provide real estate advice, negotiate, or participate in any part of a real estate transaction beyond making the referral introduction. All real estate services are provided by a licensed real estate sales agent.
