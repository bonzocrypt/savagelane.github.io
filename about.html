About Savage Lane

Savage Lane is a real estate referral concierge. If you want to buy, sell, or rent, this site helps you request an introduction to an appropriate licensed real estate professional in your area.

About me

I am a licensed real estate Referral Agent with nearly 2 decades of experience in finance and over 7 years affiliated with the Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network. Over the years I have learned what clients care about most, clear expectations, fast communication, and working with a professional who follows the rules and does the job the right way.

Savage Lane exists for 1 purpose, to make it simple to get connected with a licensed local real estate professional through a straightforward intake and referral introduction process.

What you can expect here

  • A simple way to request a referral introduction
  • Clear disclosures about my role as a Referral Agent
  • No transaction participation beyond making the referral introduction

Disclosure: Daniel Belliveau is a licensed Referral Agent with the Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network. Savage Lane is an independent referral introduction website and is not operated by Coldwell Banker Realty Referral Network. As a Referral Agent, Daniel Belliveau does not provide real estate advice, representation, negotiation, or participate in any part of a real estate transaction beyond making the referral introduction. All real estate services are provided by licensed real estate professionals through their own brokerages.
